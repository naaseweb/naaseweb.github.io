NAASE has committed to organizing sports economics sessions at the annual meetings of three prominent scholarly associations of economists. 

- Western Economic Association International (June/July)
- Southern Economic Association (November)
- Eastern Economic Association (March)

 is held in conjunction with the annual meetings of the Western Economic Association International.

 NAASE members also often organize sessions at other regional conferences (*e.g.*,  Missouri Valley Economic Association, International Atlantic Economic Society), as well as at special conferences throughout the world. We provide a list of upcoming gatherings of sports economists, along with session organizers. Please contact NAASE officers if you wish NAASE to promote an upcoming meeting of of sports economists that is not listed below.
