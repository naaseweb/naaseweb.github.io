The annual meeting of NAASE is held in conjunction with the annual meetings of the Western Economic Association International.

NAASE regularly schedules sessions at several annual economics conferences including the Western Economic Association Conference (held in July), the Eastern Economic Association Conference (held in March), and the Southern Economic Association Conference (held in November). Sessions are also often held at other regional conferences including the Missouri Valley Economic Association (held in October)and the International Atlantic Economic Society (held in October), and the Western Economic Association International Conference (generally held in January in Pacific Rim countries). If you are interested in presenting a sports economics paper at one of these conferences, please contact the relevant session organizers listed below.
